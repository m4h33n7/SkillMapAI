# SkillMap AI

**Map your skills. Navigate your career.**

SkillMap AI is a web app that helps you discover job matches based on your skills, explore roles by industry, and build a personalized skill roadmap.

## Features

- **Skill Matcher** — Enter your skills and get instant job recommendations with compatibility scores and skill gaps
- **Job Explorer** — Browse 1,167+ job listings across 11 categories (HR, IT, Finance, Sales, Business Development, Marketing, Engineering, Operations, Customer Service, Legal, Healthcare)
- **Skill Roadmap** — Choose a target category and see the most in-demand skills to learn, with optional filtering by your current skills
